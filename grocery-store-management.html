Project Overview

I built this Grocery Store Management System to streamline the complex operations of local retail stores. By integrating a Flask backend with a MySQL relational database, I created an end-to-end solution that handles everything from inventory tracking to automated billing, ensuring store owners have full control over their business data.

Core Features
📦

Inventory Control

Live stock monitoring with automated database updates whenever a sale is made.

🧾

Billing System

Automated price calculations and transaction storage for instant receipt generation.

👥

Customer Records

Centralized storage for customer contact info and comprehensive purchase history.

📊

Sales Tracking

Daily sales dashboards that help store owners monitor revenue and product performance.
