Objective

I built this Personal Finance Tracker to help people take control of their financial life. It’s a full-stack application that allows users to log income and expenses, view spending patterns through visual charts, and manage their budget securely from any device.

Key Features

Income Tracking

Log salaries, bonuses, or side-hustle income with ease.

Categorized Spending

Assign expenses to categories like Travel, Food, or Utilities.

Secure Dashboard

A private space protected by login to view your financial data.

Real-time Summaries

Instantly see your total balance and monthly spending progress.
